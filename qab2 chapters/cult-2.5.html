"Quirkless Activism"

by Shuttai on September 04, 20X4 - 331 Comments

 

There's been a lot of discussion about my political views, and my so-called "quirkless activism" comes up quite a bit. I don't consider myself an activist per-say, but I see quirklessness as an issue society largely ignores. In a world where the first field after your full name is a quirk, erasure is a larger issue than you might consider. When applying online for a high school entrance exam, did the 'quirk type' field give you any pause? Did you notice there were only three options; emitter, transformation, and mutant? This is a required field of entry for forms you'll fill out for your entire life. Especially in forms geared towards the younger generation (i.e., high school and college applications), forms must be competed online and they're missing something important: a fourth option, for those without quirks.
