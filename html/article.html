Vigilantism in the Age of Heroes Hero school graduation rates are higher than ever, so why the increase in vigilantism?

BY TOKUDA TANEO, @TOKUDATANE


Rates for hero school attendance are up 12% from 5 years ago, and up 35% from 10 years ago. For the last 20 years, public opinions on heroes have done nothing but rise. This begs the question: why the sudden increase in vigilante activity?
