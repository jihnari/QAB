UA Student Housing Proposal Denied
by Kuwahara Chieko
August 18, 2X04

Voters in the UA Community School District rejected a ballot proposal for on-campus student housing in a special election held on Wednesday.

Votes for measure approval fell 20.5 percent short of the simple majority needed.

If approved, the students currently attending UA would be required to use the new boarding system in order to remain enrolled. The failure of this measure to pass is largely attributed to UAs overall failure to secure their own campus and assure the safety of their students and staff during off-campus activities.
